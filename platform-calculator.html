Formula reference

Gross Revenue = Rides × Weighted Avg Ride Value
Stripe Cost = Gross × Stripe % + Rides × Stripe Fixed Fee
Platform Fee = Gross × Platform %
Vehicle Cost = Fleet Size × Per-Vehicle Fee
Monthly Total = Vehicle Cost + Stripe Cost + Platform Fee
Annual Final = max(sum of monthly totals, Annual Contract Minimum)
